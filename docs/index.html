I am an assistant professor in AI Graduate School at GIST. Before joining GIST, I was a Researcher at the Intelligent Convergence Research Laboratory, ETRI. I had been a postdoctoral researcher in the Computer Vision Lab. at the Seoul National University. I received my Ph.D. degree from POSTECH, under the supervision of Prof. Bohyung Han and Minsu Cho, and B.S. and M.S degrees from the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at Ewha Womans University.

My research interests lie in the intersection of computer vision, machine learning and deep learning, which include visual tracking, object detection, semantic/instance/panoptic segmentation, and learning with minimal or no supervision by human.
