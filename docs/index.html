Jeany Son

Assistant Professor | AI Graduate School | GIST

I am an associate professor in AI Graduate School at GIST. Before joining GIST, I was a Researcher at the Intelligent Convergence Research Laboratory, ETRI. I had been a postdoctoral fellow at the Seoul National University. I received my Ph.D. degree from POSTECH, under the supervision of Prof. Bohyung Han and Minsu Cho, and B.S. and M.S degrees from the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at Ewha Womans University. My research interests lie in the intersection of Computer Vision, Machine Learning and Deep Learning, especially visual tracking, segmentation and learning with least supervision.
